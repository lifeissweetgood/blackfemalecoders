Grepping for BFCs

The following survey is part of an ongoing mission of BlackFemaleCoders to find and highlight other BFCs in the world doing their thing. 'grep' is a Linux command that searches an input file for a particular pattern and returns the matches. We're using the same concept here to search the interwebs for other BFCs and show the world we exist.

We want to get to know you and hear about all of the cool things you do and are interested in!

We invite BFCs of all experience levels to participate, from the hobbyist programmer who only just started learning about development to the BFC who spends her days coding away. Whether you're a BFC studying computing, a BFC working professionally in IT/development or a BFC who only develops in her spare time, we want to hear from you! BFC is about expanding our coder community and the best way to start is by introducing yourself! :)
